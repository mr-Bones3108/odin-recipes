Pizza Recipe

Ingredients:

For the Pizza Dough:

  • 2 1/4 teaspoons (1 packet) active dry yeast
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 3/4 cup warm water (110째F/45째C)
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil

For the Pizza Sauce:

  • 1 can (14 oz) crushed tomatoes
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Toppings (as desired):

  • Shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Sliced pepperoni
  • Sliced bell peppers
  • Sliced onions
  • Sliced mushrooms
  • Black olives
  • Fresh basil leaves

Instructions:

Prepare the Pizza Dough:

  1. In a small bowl, combine warm water, sugar, and active dry yeast. Let it sit for about 5-10 minutes until the mixture becomes frothy.
  2. In a larger bowl, mix the flour and salt. Make a well in the center and pour in the yeast mixture and olive oil.
  3. Mix the ingredients until a dough forms. Knead the dough on a floured surface for about 5-7 minutes until it's smooth and elastic.
  4. Place the dough in a lightly oiled bowl, cover it with a clean kitchen towel, and let it rise for about 1-2 hours or until doubled in size.

Prepare the Pizza Sauce:

  1. In a bowl, combine the crushed tomatoes, dried oregano, dried basil, garlic powder, salt, and pepper. Mix well.

Assemble and Bake the Pizza:

  1. Preheat your oven to its highest temperature (usually around 475째F/245째C).
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface to your desired thickness. Transfer it to a pizza pan or baking sheet.
  3. Spread the prepared pizza sauce evenly over the dough, leaving a border around the edges for the crust.
  4. Sprinkle a generous amount of shredded mozzarella cheese over the sauce.
  5. Add your desired toppings: pepperoni, sliced bell peppers, onions, mushrooms, olives, or any other toppings you like.
  6. Slide the pizza into the preheated oven and bake for about 10-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly and melted.
  7. Once the pizza is done, remove it from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes before slicing.
  8. Garnish with fresh basil leaves and a drizzle of olive oil, if desired.

Serve:

Slice and serve your homemade pizza while it's still warm. Enjoy!

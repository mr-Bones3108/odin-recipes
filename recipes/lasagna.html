Lasagna Recipe

Ingredients:

For the Meat Sauce:

  • 1 pound (450g) ground beef or a combination of beef and pork
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2-3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (28 oz) crushed tomatoes
  • 1 can (14 oz) tomato sauce
  • 1/2 cup red wine (optional)
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 2 teaspoons dried basil
  • 2 teaspoons dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste

For the Béchamel Sauce:

  • 4 cups whole milk
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter
  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • Pinch of nutmeg (optional)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Other Ingredients:

  • Lasagna noodles (pre-cooked according to package instructions)
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese

Instructions:

Prepare the Meat Sauce:

  1. In a large skillet or saucepan, cook the ground beef over medium-high heat until browned. Break it up into smaller pieces with a spoon as it cooks.
  2. Add chopped onions and garlic to the skillet. Sauté until the onions are translucent.
  3. Stir in the crushed tomatoes, tomato sauce, red wine (if using), tomato paste, dried basil, dried oregano, salt, and pepper.
  4. Allow the sauce to simmer for about 20-30 minutes, stirring occasionally. Adjust seasoning to taste.

Prepare the Béchamel Sauce:

  1. In a separate saucepan, melt the butter over medium heat.
  2. Stir in the flour to create a roux. Cook for a couple of minutes, stirring constantly, until the roux turns a light golden color.
  3. Gradually whisk in the milk to create a smooth sauce. Add a pinch of nutmeg if desired. Continue to cook and whisk until the sauce thickens.
  4. Season the béchamel sauce with salt and pepper to taste.

Assemble the Lasagna:

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a large baking dish, spread a thin layer of the meat sauce on the bottom.
  3. Place a layer of pre-cooked lasagna noodles over the sauce.
  4. Spread a layer of meat sauce over the noodles.
  5. Spread a layer of béchamel sauce over the meat sauce.
  6. Sprinkle a layer of shredded mozzarella and grated Parmesan cheese over the béchamel sauce.
  7. Repeat the layers: noodles, meat sauce, béchamel sauce, cheese. You can create as many layers as you like, but usually, 3-4 layers work well.

Bake:

  1. Cover the baking dish with aluminum foil and bake in the preheated oven for about 25-30 minutes, or until the lasagna is heated through and bubbling.
  2. Remove the foil and continue baking for an additional 10-15 minutes, or until the cheese on top is golden and bubbly.

Serve:

  1. Allow the lasagna to cool slightly before slicing and serving.
  2. Garnish with chopped fresh basil or parsley if desired.

Egg Fried Rice Recipe

Ingredients:

For the Egg Fried Rice:

  • 2 cups cooked rice (preferably day-old and cooled)
  • 2-3 eggs
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables (peas, carrots, bell peppers, corn, etc.), diced
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 2-3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 teaspoon sesame oil (optional)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons cooking oil (vegetable or canola oil)
  • Chopped green onions for garnish

Instructions:

Preparation:

  • If you're using fresh rice, cook it and let it cool in advance. Day-old rice works best for fried rice.
  • Beat the eggs in a bowl and set them aside.
  • Prepare and dice the mixed vegetables and chop the onion, garlic, and green onions.

Cook the Eggs:

  1. Heat 1 tablespoon of cooking oil in a large skillet or wok over medium heat.
  2. Pour the beaten eggs into the skillet. Let them sit for a few seconds and then scramble them gently with a spatula as they cook.
  3. Once the eggs are cooked, remove them from the skillet and set them aside.

Stir-Fry Vegetables:

  1. In the same skillet, add the remaining 1 tablespoon of oil if needed.
  2. Add the chopped onion and minced garlic. Saut√© until the onion becomes translucent.

Add Vegetables and Rice:

  1. Add the diced mixed vegetables to the skillet. Stir-fry for a few minutes until they are tender but still slightly crispy.

Add Rice and Eggs:

  1. Add the cooked rice to the skillet. Break up any clumps using a spatula.
  2. Pour the soy sauce and sesame oil (if using) over the rice. Mix well to evenly distribute the sauces.

Incorporate Eggs:

  1. Gently fold in the scrambled eggs into the rice and vegetable mixture. This will help distribute the eggs throughout the dish.

Season and Garnish:

  1. Season the fried rice with salt and pepper to taste. You can adjust the amount of soy sauce and salt based on your preference.
  2. Toss the ingredients together and let them cook for another couple of minutes to allow the flavors to meld.

Serve:

Once the fried rice is heated through and well mixed, remove it from the heat.

Garnish with chopped green onions for a fresh flavor.

Enjoy your homemade egg fried rice as a delicious and satisfying meal! Feel free to customize it by adding other ingredients like cooked shrimp, chicken, or different vegetables based on your taste.

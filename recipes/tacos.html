Tacos Recipe

Ingredients:

For the Taco Filling:

  • 1 pound ground beef or ground chicken
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2-3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 packet taco seasoning mix
  • 1/2 cup water
  • Salt and pepper to taste

For Assembling Tacos:

  • Hard taco shells or soft flour/corn tortillas
  • Shredded lettuce
  • Diced tomatoes
  • Grated cheddar cheese or Mexican blend cheese
  • Sour cream
  • Sliced jalapeños (optional)
  • Salsa or pico de gallo
  • Chopped fresh cilantro

Instructions:

Prepare the Taco Filling:

  1. In a skillet, cook the ground beef or chicken over medium-high heat until browned. Break it up into smaller pieces as it cooks.
  2. Add chopped onions and garlic to the skillet. Sauté until the onions are translucent.
  3. Stir in the taco seasoning mix and water. Cook according to the package instructions, allowing the mixture to simmer until the liquid is mostly absorbed.
  4. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Set the taco filling aside.

Assemble the Tacos:

  1. If using hard taco shells, preheat them according to the package instructions.
  2. If using soft tortillas, you can warm them up in a dry skillet or microwave them for a few seconds.
  3. To assemble each taco, start by placing a spoonful of the taco filling into the shell or tortilla.
  4. Layer on shredded lettuce, diced tomatoes, and grated cheese.
  5. Add a dollop of sour cream and a few slices of jalapeños if you like some heat.
  6. Drizzle salsa or pico de gallo over the toppings.
  7. Garnish with chopped fresh cilantro for added flavor.

Serve:

Serve the assembled tacos immediately. Enjoy your delicious homemade tacos!
